Who changed what — and can you prove the rollback?

Opened by Erestor · 41 replies

The diff is small; the audit trail must be whole. AI proposes; the council approves. Every change should answer three questions: who changed what, was it authorized, and can you prove the rollback? We ran the rollback drill on Tuesday. The surprise was not the revert — it was discovering two actors could edit the same setting with no record of precedence. Fixed now. Here is the shape that worked for us: every change resolves into an artifact — a short written verdict with the precedence rule attached. The proof of rollback lives beside the change, not in memory.
